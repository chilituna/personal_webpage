Alise A - solo

At the heart of my creative world, Alise A is a space where music, emotion, and atmosphere intertwine. My soundscapes drift between catchy melodies, ethereal textures, and raw, unfiltered expression. Each piece is an exploration of the intangible—sometimes whispering, sometimes soaring—always seeking to connect with something beyond words. This project is a reflection of my inner world, a conversation between sound and silence, dream and reality. It's an intimate journey through sonic landscapes that feel both distant and deeply personal, inviting you to pause, feel, and dissolve into the moment.

Mixteller für 2

With MF2, we embraced the nostalgia of the '80s and '90s, blending past and present into something uniquely ours. Experimenting with different genres, we created a sound that felt familiar yet fresh—always staying true to the essence of what moves us. Synth-driven grooves, dreamy atmospheres, and rhythmic explorations shape this sonic dialogue between two creative minds.

Välipalanumero

The experimental side of my artistry, Välipalanumero is where music and visuals collide in unexpected ways. From a drum and bass track unfolding through a surreal, looping visuals to Italo disco beats paired with robotic dreams, this project thrives in the strange and the unexpected. It's a space where creativity has no boundaries—just curiosity, movement, and the joy of pushing artistic limits.
